Fun fact: Franz Schubert's "D" number refers to the Deutsch catalogue, a chronological listing of all his works compiled by musicologist Otto Erich Deutsch (1883–1967). Mainly because Because Schubert lived a short life and wrote many works that were not published immediately, Opus numbers are insufficient/misleading for chronological ordering.
