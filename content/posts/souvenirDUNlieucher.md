> “Memory of a dear place” is the title given to this collection of three enchanting pieces for violin and piano. **The “place” in question was the country estate of Brailov that belonged to Tchaikovsky's patron and friend Nadezhda von Meck**.
>
> [Hal Leonard Publication](https://www.halleonard.com/product/51481275/souvenir-dun-lieu-cher-op-42#)
