This is gonna be a never-ending debate. But here's what's happening right now, according to folklore

Lyft claim that **Lyft fee** is capped at 30\%. What they mean by it, according to Lyft's blog, is

Rider pay = **Insurance + Taxes + Gov Fees** + *Lyft's fee* + Driver's Income

**Insurance + Taxes + Gov Fees** are "External fees" and does NOT count into those 30%.
