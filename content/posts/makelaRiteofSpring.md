The CSO curates program with glittering wisdom. A while ago back November, Robert Chen led the strings presented Vivaldi's *Four Seasons* and presented a beautiful *winter*. And now, our new artistic director led an adorable (and impressive) program welcoming spring:

> Milhaud *Le Bœuf sur le toit*
>
> Gershwin *An American in Paris*
>
> Stravinsky *The Rite of Spring*

{{<figure align="center" width="100%" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/aritang/aritangPictures/main/static/art/makelaRite.jpeg" caption="">}}

I got lucky to sit *facing right at the cello/bass* section. The Rite of Spring's most impressive rhythm are carried out by them and it was absolutely a treat to hear the song hit straight at you.

Two sold out nights. The concert program is nice and covers stories about the music: https://issuu.com/chicagosymphony/docs/program_book_-_m_kel_conducts_the_rite_of_spring
