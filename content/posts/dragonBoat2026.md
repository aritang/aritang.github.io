It's my favourite time of the year, and today is the *Dragon Boat Festival* (of 2026)! For this year I want to quote '同舟而济':

> 夫吴人与越人相恶也，当其同舟而济，遇风，其相救也如左右手。《孙子兵法·九地》
>
> Then the men of Wu and the men of Yüeh bear one another enmity; yet, should they cross the same river in one boat and be caught by a storm, they will fly to each other's aid as the left hand succours the right. — *Sun Tzu, The Art of War*, Chapter XI ("The Nine Situations")

May the spirit and luck be with us!
